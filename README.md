# SAPRES SARL Website & Management Platform

A full-stack corporate website, e-commerce catalog, recruitment portal, and administration platform for SAPRES SARL, a leading solar energy solutions company and official partner of Blue Carbon.

## Overview

This platform is designed to showcase SAPRES SARL's solar energy products, services, projects, and company information while providing powerful business management capabilities through a secure administration dashboard.

The system enables customers to browse products, request quotations, submit recruitment applications, make payments via MTN Mobile Money and Orange Money, and contact the company directly through WhatsApp.
